Welcome!

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Mathematical Sciences at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). Previously I was a Van Vleck visiting assistant professor at the University of Wisconsin – Madison. Before that I was a postdoctoral research fellow at the University of Warwick. I got my PhD in the Applied and Interdisciplinary Mathematics program at the University of Michigan under the supervision of Jinho Baik.

I obtained my BSc from the School of the Gifted Young, University of Science and Technology of China, specializing in Mathematics.

My general research interests lie in probability theory and mathematical physics. I have been working on problems in integrable probability, interacting particle systems, random matrices and SPDEs.
